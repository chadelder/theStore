Lombardy Market was opened on September 20, 1985 by one Ralph Eubank Watkinson from the Eastern Shore of Virgina. RE sees more humans walk in and out of the store in a year that most meet in a life time. The deli hasn't changed, high qualtiy products and a touch of love goes a long way. The LM keeps a good beer and wine selection, coffee, and VCU students are always happy. So drop bye for a sandwich, or to talk it out if you're close bye. By the way this is VCU country...so, if you're a U of R alumni, don't bother!
